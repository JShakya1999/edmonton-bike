As-is bike for sale

We offer two types of 'as-is' bikes, in addition to our fully refurbished options:

  1. Project Bikes – These bikes have not been tuned and may range from bare frames to complete bikes. Most will require additional parts or repairs before they are safe to use.
  2. Quick Tuned Bikes – These bikes are quickly repaired to ensure safety and basic functionality. While cheaper than fully refurbished bikes, they may still need further work to function optimally.

To view available 'as-is' bikes, book a sale appointment at our Community Workshop. Prices and selection vary.
