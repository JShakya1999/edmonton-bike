Become a Bike Edmonton Membe

Buying a membership supports Bike Edmonton by providing stable funding for services like bike building, sales, parts, repairs, and courses. It also strengthens the collective voice of cyclists, advocating for better, more sustainable bike infrastructure in Edmonton and other cities.

  • Discounts at businesses and events across the city.
  • Discounts on shop fees at both of our community bike workshops.
  • Discounts on bike repair courses and other workshops.
